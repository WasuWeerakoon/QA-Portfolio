## 2. Objective

To verify that the **Product Listing page** displays all products correctly and allows users to browse, view, and navigate product information without errors.

---

## 3. Assumptions

- Product listing page is accessible
- Product data is available
- User has stable internet connectivity
- No login is required to view product listings

---

## 4. Product Listing Test Scenarios

### TS-PL-01: Verify product listing page loads successfully

- Ensure the product listing page opens without errors.

### TS-PL-02: Verify all products are displayed

- Ensure all available products are listed on the page.

### TS-PL-03: Verify product image display

- Ensure each product has a visible and properly loaded image.

### TS-PL-04: Verify product name display

- Ensure product names are displayed correctly and are readable.

### TS-PL-05: Verify product price display

- Ensure product prices are displayed correctly.

### TS-PL-06: Verify product category display

- Ensure product categories are displayed where applicable.

### TS-PL-07: Verify product card UI consistency

- Ensure product cards are aligned properly and UI is consistent.

### TS-PL-08: Verify navigation to product details page

- Ensure clicking a product navigates to the correct product details page.

### TS-PL-09: Verify product listing page scroll behavior

- Ensure the page scrolls smoothly and loads all products.

### TS-PL-10: Verify product listing page responsiveness

- Ensure the product listing page is responsive on mobile devices.

### TS-PL-11: Verify product listing on different browsers

- Ensure product listing works correctly on Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.

### TS-PL-12: Verify product listing page performance (basic)

- Ensure the page loads within an acceptable time under normal conditions.

---

## 5. Expected Outcome

- Product listing page should load successfully
- All products should be displayed with correct information
- Images, names, and prices should be visible and accurate
- Navigation to product details should work correctly
- UI should be consistent across browsers and devices
